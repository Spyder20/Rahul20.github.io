Cybersecurity Ethics - Hackers

The page or area describing the choices available to us through or by your chosen technology/topic.

Types of Hackers

Colours are used as general categories to describe the wide range of hackers.
-White (Positive)
-Black (Negative)
-Grey (Neutral)

Hacking Ethics

-Ethics of a hacker can be one of the benchmarks to tell if a hacker is good or bad. (What their motivation, permission and legality is)
-An ethical hacker can assist a company in determining where the most important data is kept and how the best way to secure it. This process secures the collected data.
-Collaboration with a company to help reduce the risk of security breaches from black hat hackers. This proves that this is work of a white hat hacker.

Specific Tasks

White Hat: (Security Engineer, Security Analyst, Ethical Hacker, Penetration Tester)
Black Hat: (Cyber Criminal, Cyber Spies, Cyber Terrorist, Hacktivists)
Grey Hat: (Cyber Vandalism, Rogue Security Software Developer)

Technology Opportunities (Cyber Security)

Cybersecurity Ethics
-Protection of individuals or businesses against the breach of privacy, identity theft and interference of work.
-Cybersecurity practices can significantly impact each of these fundamental interests of human beings.

Prevent Misuse of Personal Information
-Our information is spread online through shopping, registering accounts and advertisement tracking.
-Sensitive data that is stored rarely stays protected and hidden where it was originally created due to hackers.
-Hacking and other network intrusions can also be used to obtain sensitive information about individuals and their
activities can be used for the purposes of blackmail, extortion and other forms of unethical illegal manipulation.

Prevent Theft of Intellectual Property
 -There are many parts of the internet that consist of various intellectual property such as music, song artists and so on.
-Without presence of computer ethics, the work created can easily be plagiarized by someone else.

Keep Your Computer Safe
-Our computer is not just an electronic device for communication, it stores private information and all sorts of other data.
-If our system is used in an ethical way, we can rest assured that our work and personal information is safe and secure.
